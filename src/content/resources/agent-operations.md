Agent operations covers the practical work of running AI agents inside product and engineering workflows. It includes task admission, permissions, state, observability, retry behavior, and human review.

The community treats agents as operational systems, not magic boxes. A useful agent has clear boundaries, visible logs, recoverable failures, and a way for people to take back control.

## What Members Share

- Patterns for agent queues, ownership, and status transitions.
- Guardrails for filesystem, API, browser, and deployment access.
- Review workflows that keep changes understandable.
- Lessons from long-running and multi-agent tasks.

## Why It Matters

As agents move beyond chat into action, operational design becomes the difference between a useful assistant and a risky automation. VibeOps gives builders a place to compare what is working.
